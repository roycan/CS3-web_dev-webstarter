📚 Instructions for High School Students

How to Change Your Website's Colors

Step 1: Open your styles.css file in your text editor.

Step 2: Find the body section that looks like this:

body {
  margin: 0;
  font-family: cursive, system-ui, sans-serif;
  color: royalblue;
  background-color: cornsilk;
}

Step 3: Replace the color names with your chosen colors:

  • background-color: This changes the background of your entire page
  • color: This changes the text color for most of your content

Example Changes:

To make your page have a light blue background with dark blue text:

body {
  margin: 0;
  font-family: cursive, system-ui, sans-serif;
  color: navy;
  background-color: lightblue;
}

To make your page have a lavender background with purple text:

body {
  margin: 0;
  font-family: cursive, system-ui, sans-serif;
  color: purple;
  background-color: lavender;
}

💡 Pro Tips:

  • Always save your file after making changes
  • Refresh your browser to see the changes
  • Make sure your text color contrasts well with your background color so it's easy to read
  • You can use any of the color names shown above, or search online for "CSS color names" to find more options
  • Light backgrounds work best with dark text colors
  • Dark backgrounds work best with light text colors
